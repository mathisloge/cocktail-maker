= Quality Requirements

This chapter refines the machine-wide quality goals in xref:system::quality-goals.adoc[] into concrete
scenarios for the Station application.

[cols="1,3", options="header"]
|===
| Quality Goal | Station-Level Scenario

| *Human Safety*
| A guest or operator can stop any in-progress dispensing at any time, and the Station brings every
connected Pod into a safe state rather than leaving an actuator running.

| *Food Safety*
| An emptied dispenser is detected and reported before more of a recipe is dispensed from it, instead
of continuing with a partial or wrong pour.

| *Reliability*
| A Pod that stops responding mid-exchange times out and is reported as disconnected, instead of
leaving a guest's mix in progress indefinitely.

| *Maintainability*
| Adding a new recipe, ingredient, or glass, or reassigning which dispenser serves which ingredient,
requires only a data change or an admin-UI action, not a rebuild.

| *Usability*
| A guest can complete an order using only the touch screen, with no separate instructions, and the
operator's maintenance controls stay out of their way entirely.
|===
